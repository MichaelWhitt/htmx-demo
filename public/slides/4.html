Targets and Swapping
  • Targeting:
    Specify element to receive HTML -hx-target="#elementId"
  • Swapping:
    Choose target's location to inject html Update only that part of the DOM, avoiding unnecessary repaints/reflows -hx-swap="innerHTML" (replaces inner html of element) -outerHTML (replaces entire element) -none -and more
